Hello!

My name is Alicja (uh-LEE-ts-ya) and this is my personal website.

I write about learning things and exploring ideas. In no particular order, I like Haskell, CSS, writing documentation, building systems, analyzing data, drawing, learning languages, and chocolate.

Professionally, I work at Mobify, building developer tools for progressive web apps. I am also a part of the Recurse Center - a community where programmers take time off to focus on their growth.
